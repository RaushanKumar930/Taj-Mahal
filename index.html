THE TAJ MAHAL





The Taj Mahal is a beautiful white marble monument located in the city of Agra on the banks of river Yamuna.
It is one of the finest examples of architecture that combines elements of Mughal, Persian, Ottoman Turkish and Indian architectural styles.
Located in the state of Uttar Pradesh, it one of the Seven Wonders of the World and a jewel of Muslim art in India.
It was built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife, Mumtaz.
